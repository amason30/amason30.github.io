Summary

I’m a microbial ecologist with experience in computational biology and management of multivariate projects. My research encompasses soil microbial ecology and terrestrial vertebrate decomposition. I have experience with multiple programming languages, with an expertise in R and execution of microbiome data analysis. I’m passionate about open source science, reproducible research, and teaching computational skill to individuals of all levels.
